ググらずに答えてね。
p^0*(1-p)1 + p^1(1-p)2 + p^2(1-p)3 + p^(n-1)(1-p)n
             p^1(1-p)1 + p^2(1-p)2 + p^(n-1)(1-p)(n-1) - p^n(1-p)n

s - sp = p^0*(1-p) + p^1*(1-p) + ... + p^(n-1)(1-p) - p^n(1-p)n
s = p^0 + p^1 + ... +  p^(n-1) - p^nn
s = (1 - p^n) / (1-p) - p^nn
